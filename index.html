HOW IT WORKS

Technical Phone Interview

At very first step our team will conduct a 30 mins technical interview based on the job requirements.

Coding Challenge

If the candidate pass through the first step then the candidate will be given coding challenge which will include but not limited to small Project, analytical questions etc.

Code Review

Our technical team will thoroughly evaluate the code submitted by candidate. We also discuss with the candidate to learn more about their thought on the solution.

Project Review

Our team will also evaluate any past project that candidate worked on including any contribution to open source projects. Discuss with candidate about theier coding methodology

Physical interview

If the candidate scores satisfactory result then we will send the candidate for in person interview to the hiring company.

Hire with Confident

Finally, employer will do the last set of screening to hire the candidate
